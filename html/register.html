The Cleveland Center Wellness Association is a voluntary organization whose goal is to imporove the physical and mental health and welfare of the employees of Cleveland ARTCC. The association has been granted space for a Wellness Center work-out area. Use of the equipment in the Cneter is limited to members of the Association who have signed this agreement and have paid the annual fee.

Members of teh Association agree to abide by the rules and limitations of the Association listed below. Failure to abide by these rules swhall result in forfeiture of the rightsw to use an and all equipment in the Wellness Center.

  1. The annual fee for membership in the Weellness Association is $100.00, payable in January of each year. Use of the membership fee shall be governed by the Wellness Committee and shall be governed by the equipment, or for other wellness activites. Members whose renewal fees are more than 30 days in arrears shall be terminated from the Association. Annual fee subject to change with advance notice to members.
